BMGIS Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

BMGIS is a community-supported benchmark mapping app that helps users locate and update BM reference points. This policy explains what data BMGIS collects, how it is used, and how users can request account deletion.

1. Information we collect

BMGIS may collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account information such as email address and display name.
  • Location information captured when you submit or update a BM.
  • Photos, comments, condition reports, and community-submitted BM coordinates.
  • Votes and reports submitted on community-updated BM data.
  • Account deletion requests and content reporting requests.

2. How we use the information

BMGIS uses this information to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain their session.
  • Allow users to submit benchmark updates, photos, comments, and field observations.
  • Display community-updated BM information publicly inside the app.
  • Support community voting and reporting of incorrect or inappropriate content.
  • Handle support, moderation, and account deletion requests.

3. Public community submissions

BM updates submitted by users may become publicly visible in the app. This can include submitted coordinates, photos, comments, benchmark condition, and related community update details.

JUPEM source benchmark data remains separate from community-submitted updates.

4. Location data

The app uses device location to help users find nearby BM points and to support field submissions and updates. Location is only used as part of the app’s BM mapping and update workflow.

5. Content moderation

Users can report community content such as photos, coordinates, or comments if they believe the content is wrong, abusive, inappropriate, or spam. Reported content may be reviewed and acted on.

6. Data sharing

BMGIS does not sell personal data. Community benchmark updates are shown publicly inside the app to support collaborative field verification.

7. Account deletion

Users can request account deletion from the Account screen inside the BMGIS app. For more information, see the Account Deletion page.

8. Data retention

We may retain account, submission, moderation, and operational records for a reasonable period as needed to operate the service, review reports, and process deletion requests.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact: bmgis.my@gmail.com
